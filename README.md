# Lead Ace: AI-Powered Lead Generation Platform

Lead Ace is an advanced platform that streamlines the lead generation lifecycle using Generative AI. It allows sales teams to discover, capture, and qualify leads from virtually any source.

## Key Features

### 1. Intelligent Discovery
- **Natural Language Search**: Describe your target market in plain text.
- **AI-Driven Keywords**: The system optimizes search terms to find the most relevant businesses.

### 2. Versatile Lead Capture
- **Web & Bulk URL Capture**: Extract contact details from websites or partner lists.
- **Ad & Social Capture**: Upload screenshots of advertisements or social posts to identify leads.
- **Street View Prospecting**: Explore areas via Google Street View to identify and capture physical business leads with photographic evidence.

### 3. Data Intelligence
- **Automated Enrichment**: Fetch company data including tech stacks, revenue, and funding info.
- **Predictive Scoring**: Prioritize leads based on an AI-calculated quality score.
- **AI Summaries**: Get concise, one-paragraph briefings on every lead.

### 4. Management & Analytics
- **Lead Pipeline**: Track status from discovery to qualification.
- **Interactive Dashboard**: Visualize conversion rates and lead quality.
- **CSV Export**: Easily move your leads into other CRM tools.

## Tech Stack
- **Framework**: Next.js (App Router)
- **Database/Auth**: Firebase (Firestore & Authentication)
- **AI**: Google Genkit & Gemini 1.5 Flash
- **UI**: ShadCN UI, Tailwind CSS, Lucide Icons
